Starport
The fastest way to build a blockchain: Starport's new release

Starport is the easiest way to build Cosmos blockchains in under five minutes.  It provides a rapid prototyping tool for developers to quickly launch a blockchain with just two CLI commands. Today's brand new release of Starport focused on improving its core features: scaffolding, configuring and running a blockchain development environment.

Stargate
New date and time for Stargate

The postponement of Stargate was announced last week and subsequently a new governance proposal (prop#37) was issued. The proposal sets  06:00 UTC on Feb 18th as the new date and time for the Stargate upgrade.

Inter-Blockchain Communication
A cosmic future with IBC

The Stargate upgrade is making history by enabling the Cosmos Hub to be the first cross-chain interoperable network through Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC). Among a host of other features, IBC will also have its own application development platform for fungible tokens, NFTs, Interchain Accounts and more. What does this mean for projects and developers?

Cosmos Hub
5 million blocks and beyond

The Cosmos Hub is coming up on its two year anniversary. In its lifespan it produced millions of blocks. This week was record hitting with five million on the CosmosHub-3 chain, the longest Cosmos Hub chain since Mainnet. The speed of block production is on the rise!
