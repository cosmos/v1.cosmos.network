The Graph Protocol integrates with Cosmos

The Graph Protocol makes it easy to query and index blockchain data without complex infrastructure. Cosmos developers will be able to use the Graph’s subgraph support to conduct fast and reliable on-chain data operations. 

Long time Cosmos contributor, Figment, is taking the lead on bringing the Graph to the Cosmos ecosystem. “Figment and The Graph have a shared belief that the future of web3 is multi-chain. Bringing The Graph to Cosmos helps IBC enabled networks to be indexed and queried in a decentralized manner, allowing developers access to blockchain data without having to manage their own infrastructure.”
