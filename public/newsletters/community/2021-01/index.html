Stargate is Happening
The Gateway to Inter-Blockchain Communication

Stargate is the codename for the technical stack developed to bring IBC to Cosmos SDK based chains. 

ATOM token holders, validators and delegators, wallets, explorers, and dApps should all be paying attention.

A governance proposal has been put forth to the community to bring Stargate to Cosmos Hub. The voting period lasts until January 26th with the ecosystem-wide upgrade taking place on the 28th.
