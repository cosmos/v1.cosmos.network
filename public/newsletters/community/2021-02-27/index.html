The Cosmos Hub is a port city

DeFi
Tgrade: A DeFi proof-of-engagement blockchain

The newly launched Tgrade is a DeFi blockchain application backed by Proof of Engagement (PoE) governance for regulated decentralized finance. Developers can use Tgrade to deploy DeFi products, such as pegs and automated market makers, using the robust CosmWasm engine.

Tgrade's mission is to advance and secure the development of new financial instruments with smart contracts. This allows the DeFi world access to an open flow of liquidity from traditional finance and regulated markets through governance. Tgrade aims to expand financial innovation opportunities for the Cosmos ecosystem by enabling a stable (and secure) governance structure with PoE, PoA and PoS combined.

      Stargate
      Post-Stargate update

      Thank you to the community for its patience during the Stargate Upgrade. It was a massive coordination effort. Afterwards, there were (and are) some kinks to iron out.

      Cosmos wallets that have upgraded to Cosmos Hub-4  and are now Stargate compatible are: Keplr, Cosmosstation, imToken and Atomic Wallet. Trust Wallet, Lunie and Exodus upgrades are in the works.

      If you have issues related to one of your wallet providers, please make sure you reach out for help.

      Exchanges and Block Explorers are at different stages in the upgrade. It could be possible that there will be some lingering issues.

      If you have questions or concerns, you can always hop on the Cosmos Discord.
      Gravity - Ethereum Cosmos
      How does Gravity work?

      Althea, the team working on Gravity, the bridge from Ethereum to Cosmos, posts a clear and straightforward take on how the technology will work. A quick snippet - "Gravity lets people transfer tokens from Ethereum to Cosmos and back again by locking up tokens on the Ethereum side, and minting equivalent tokens on the Cosmos side. Unlike many bridges, Gravity is completely non-custodial- you only need to trust in the security of the Cosmos chain, not some third party bridge administrators who could run off with your funds."
