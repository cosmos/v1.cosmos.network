Welcome to the era of interchain security

Simply put, interchain security "will allow Cosmos chains to lease security to each other" - validators on one chain will be able to provide their security to other chains. For example, validators on the  Cosmos Hub, can act as security providers to other chains by leasing their services. This allows new or smaller chains to spin up quickly with access to high-level, tested security provided by the Cosmos Hub. Interchain security is bringing decentralized security to Cosmos and to the larger blockchain space.

First NFT transfer between 4 blockchains

The long time Cosmos contributor, IRISNet, launched the first-ever multi-chain transfer of NFTs to Ethereum from various Cosmos chains. The NFTs represented digital twins of traditional fine art Chinese paintings by Rongbaozhai (Studio of Glorious Treasures), a historic and world-renowned Chinese national brand dating back almost 350 years.

Attention ATOM holders: Cosmos governance proposal #59

The next major software upgrade, Vega, is being put to an on-chain vote (prop#59) ending December 12th. The upgrade consists of many updates with key features including: the Fee grant module that will allow paying fees on behalf of another account, and the Authz module, which will provide governance functions to execute transactions on behalf of another account.

Cast your vote via IBCWallet (Cosmostation), Keplr Wallet and Rainbow Wallet.
