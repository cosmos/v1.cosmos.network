Stargate: A major Cosmos upgrade


The Stargate project represents the biggest upgrade event in the Cosmos ecosystem since mainnet launch. Stargate is a massive cross-team undertaking and primarily includes inter-blockchain communication (IBC) functionality, Protobuf compatibility and additional improvements to the Cosmos Stack.


Overall, the collective upgrade means higher transaction throughput, cross-chain transactions, simplicity for serialization, tools to accelerate UI development, and more.



Who is impacted by Stargate?


Block explorers, wallets, exchanges, validators, or any other service (eg. custody provider) that depends upon the Cosmos Hub or Cosmos ecosystem

Strategic plans for Tendermint Inc in the Cosmos ecosystem 


The Tendermint Inc team shared its short-term and long-term direction and focus in the Cosmos ecosystem. One of the first actions taken was the formation of a new Board of Directors and the election of a new CEO. This was coupled with a project to decentralize Cosmos code repositories and re-delegate ATOMs to Cosmos validators based on their contributions to Cosmos. 


The longer term strategy deals with four main targets: 

  • Enhancing the Cosmos developer experience through better tooling, tutorials, and on-boarding.
  • Hosting community events, educational resources, and programs to support Cosmos ecosystem development.
  • Lowering the barrier to entry for Cosmos technology adoption by new developers.
  • Developing innovative, easy-to-use, and community-centric Web 3.0 products and applications to improve peopleâ€™s everyday lives.
