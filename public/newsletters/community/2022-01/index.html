Bridging Ethereum and Cosmos ecosystems

The Evmos team recently released a way to instantly convert Ethereum's ERC20 tokens into native Cosmos Coins and vice versa. The Evmos module removes the asset exchange barrier between two different blockchain layers, an EVM and Cosmos. This is a big step in the interconnection of the two growing ecosystems. Find out details about their token model, the new release and what the team has in store!

Cosmos Hub
Cosmos Hub Theta upgrade is happening in Q1

The community is talking about the upcoming Theta Upgrade and the many new features that are coming to the Cosmos Hub like interchain accounts and liquid staking. It also includes potential NFT functionality for the Cosmos Hub through the Cosmos SDK NFT module which will include collectibles, custody, provenance, and marketplaces for Cosmos-based projects. 

The Theta Upgrade features list, future upgrade timelines and considerations are all available under the Cosmos Hub Github repo. Stay tuned for more details on this exciting release!
